A coding competition designed to challenge your skills and reward creativity. Submit solutions via Google Forms to win prizes and earn academic plus points while joining a vibrant student developer community.

Feedback & Evaluation

Continuous improvement, always.

Every program we run is measured, evaluated, and improved — because CCS students deserve events and services that get better every time.

  • 01
    Pre-Evaluation
    Gather student insights and expectations before every event to ensure programs are designed around real needs.
  • 02
    Post-Evaluation
    Honest feedback collected after every program to identify what worked, what did not, and how to improve next time.
  • 03
    Student Involvement
    Students are not just participants — they are co-designers of the programs that serve them.
CCS Nexus Passport

Your digital identity in CCS.

A smart student passport that puts access, attendance, and perks right in your hands — powered by QR technology.

Instant QR Verification
One scan confirms your identity at any CCS event — fast, contactless, and secure.
Attendance Tracking
Automatic, tamper-proof attendance logging for assemblies, seminars, and college events.
Perk Redemption
Earn and redeem rewards, discounts, and exclusive CCS benefits directly through your Nexus Passport.
